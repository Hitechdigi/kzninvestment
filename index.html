About the Forum

Why the Investment Forum?

The KwaZulu-Natal Investment Forum is a strategic platform designed to unlock pension fund capital to accelerate economic growth in the province by bringing together retirement funds, asset managers, project sponsors, DFI’s and government.

The Forum showcases bankable projects across infrastructure, energy, housing, and small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), providing a transparent view of opportunities and addressing key barriers such as policy uncertainty. By reinforcing alignment with the National Development Plan and the KZN Provincial Growth Strategy, the event builds investor confidence and promotes long-term, sustainable development.

Ultimately, the Forum positions KwaZulu-Natal as a prime destination for high-impact investments, creating a pipeline for economic growth, job creation, and inclusive development.
